My Prompt Maker

      
基於 Anthropic、Google Gemini、OpenAI 三大官方指南的提示詞生成框架。v1.1 · 2026-05-02

    


    

      
Gemini 官方框架：PTCF

      

        
元素說明範例

        
          
P · Persona設定 AI 扮演的角色與專業背景You are a marketing manager at a B2B SaaS company

          
T · Task明確說明要執行的任務（一次一件事）Draft a press release announcing our new product launch

          
C · Context提供相關背景、限制與受眾資訊The audience is tech journalists; keep it under 400 words

          
F · Format指定輸出格式、長度、結構Use standard press release format with headline, dateline, and quote
